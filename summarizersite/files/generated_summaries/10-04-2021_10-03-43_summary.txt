Food and nutrition are the way that we get fuel providing energy for our bodies.
For pregnant women and adults over 50 vitamins such as vitamin D and minerals such as calcium and iron are important to consider when choosing foods to eat as well as possible dietary supplements.
A healthy diet includes a lot of natural foods.
Protein can consist of lean meat and poultry seafood eggs beans legumes and soy products such as tofu as well as unsalted seeds and nuts.
Good nutrition also involves avoiding certain kinds of foods.
