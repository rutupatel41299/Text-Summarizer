Solutions are defined as liquid preparations containing one or more chemical substances usually dissolved in water.
A solution is a mixture of two compounds namely solute and solvent.
Solute is the small component present in the solution and is usually nonvolatile in nature.
Therefore special methods are used to prepare solutions.
