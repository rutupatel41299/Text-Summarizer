About

Information overloading is a big problem for fetching important information. There is huge amount of data is existed over internet and on the other resources. For human beings it is tedious to read all the data and find the accurate information. So there is a need of an automatic text summarizer which provides machine generated summaries of original data.

However, this summarizer will help students, teachers, professional, readers and many people.

How It Works?

Here, our proposed approach strengthens its process to perform the summarization of document along with the keywords identified by using text mining techniques. Since text summarization process is highly dependent on keyword extraction, the overall results are found promising. The main aim of proposed system is “condensing source text into a shorter version, while preserving its information content and overall meaning”.
